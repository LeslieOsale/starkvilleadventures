Detailed Timetable for Your Trading Journey

This timetable balances education, practice, live trading, and gradual scaling while keeping your targets, risks, and motivations in mind.

Phase 1: Education & Paper Trading

Duration: January 11 - February 15

Goal: Build knowledge and confidence in trading without risking real money.

Week Focus Area Tasks Notes
Week 1 Getting Familiar - Explore Binance, TradingView, and 3Commas.
- Watch tutorials on basic crypto trading strategies.
- Start learning candlestick patterns (e.g., Doji, Hammer). 		Motivation: Every master was once a beginner!
Week 2 Learn Indicators - Understand Moving Averages, RSI, and Bollinger Bands.
- Backtest indicators using historical data on TradingView. 		Reminder: Don’t overwhelm yourself—focus on 2-3 indicators that complement your style.
Week 3 Risk Management Basics - Learn position sizing: how much to trade per setup.
- Study stop-loss and take-profit strategies.
- Start creating a trading journal template (Excel or app). 		Risk: Without stop-losses, even good trades can turn into big losses.
Week 4 Demo Trading - Use 3Commas or Binance Paper Trading to place demo trades.
- Focus on spotting trends and applying stop-losses correctly. 		Target: Aim for at least 10 demo trades per week.

Phase 2: Starting Small (Live Trading)

Duration: February 15 - April 1

Goal: Begin live trading with minimal risk and aim for consistent small profits.

Week Focus Area Tasks Notes
Week 1 Setting Up - Deposit small starting capital ($100-$200).
- Link Binance to 3Commas for basic bot trading or manual trades. 		Reminder: Start with money you can afford to lose!
Week 2 Testing Strategies - Trade using your chosen strategy (e.g., scalping or swing trading).
- Log each trade in your journal with entry/exit, profit/loss, and analysis. 		Motivation: Small wins build confidence; losses are lessons!

Phase 3: Gradual Scaling

Duration: April 2 - December 31

Goal: Scale up trading while targeting consistent monthly growth to fund business goals and save for your car.

Month Focus Area Tasks Notes
April - June Building Consistency - Reinvest some profits to slightly increase position sizes.
- Split trades: 70% low-risk strategies, 30% high-risk trades. 		Target: Aim for 5-10% portfolio growth monthly.
July - August Diversification - Explore altcoins with higher volatility for faster growth.
- Test new bots or algorithms on 3Commas. 		Challenge: Volatile coins can offer big wins but carry higher risk. Stay disciplined!
