Starkville Adventures Refund Policy

We value your trust in our adventures and aim to provide flexible options should your plans change.

1. Reservation and Payment

  • A booking fee secures your spot.
  • Final payments must be completed by the payment deadline.

2. Refund Eligibility

  • 48-Hour Refund Window: Payments are 90% refundable up to 48 hours before the registration deadline.
  • After Deadline: Payments become non-refundable but can be redeemed for future Starkville Adventures events.

3. No-Show and Late Arrivals

  • No refunds or credits are offered for participants who miss or arrive late to the event.

4. Event Completion

  • Refunds or credits cannot be issued if a participant cannot complete the event due to personal factors like fitness or other non-company-related reasons.

Refund Requests
