Starkville Adventures - FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of adventures does Starkville Adventures offer?

We offer a variety of adventures including hiking, camping, game drives, nature trails, and team-building events. Our goal is to connect you with nature while offering exciting outdoor activities to suit all types of adventurers.

How can I book an adventure?

You can easily book your adventure on our website. Simply choose the adventure package you’re interested in, master the price and calculate booking price as 40% of the total. then click the whatsapp button for safer modes of payment. Always ask for national id proof of the staff handling your bookings. If you need help, feel free to reach out to our support team info@starkvilleadventures.co.ke.

Do I need to bring my own equipment for the activities?

For most activities, we provide the necessary equipment. However, for some specialized activities, we recommend checking our equipment list to know if you need to bring anything specific.

Can I get a discount if I bring a group?

Yes, we offer group discounts! The more people in your group, the bigger the discount. Contact us in advance to arrange for group bookings, and we’ll provide you with a special price based on the number of people attending.

How do I become a member of Starkville Adventures?

Becoming a member is simple! Our membership portal is under development, but once it’s up, you’ll be able to sign up directly through our website. Members enjoy exclusive discounts, early access to bookings, and additional benefits. Stay tuned for more updates!

What happens if I have to cancel my booking?

We recommend you to read our refund poilicy on the footer.

Is there an age limit for participating in the adventures?

Our adventures are designed to be enjoyed by all ages, though some activities may have age restrictions for safety reasons. Children must be accompanied by an adult, and certain adventures may have a minimum age requirement for participation. Please check the details of each package before booking.

Can I bring my pet on the adventures?

We love pets! However, due to safety and environmental concerns, pets are only allowed on select adventures. Please contact us in advance if you wish to bring your pet, and we’ll inform you about the available options.

What should I wear for an adventure?

We recommend comfortable and weather-appropriate clothing for all our adventures. For hiking and outdoor activities, closed-toe shoes are essential, and it’s best to wear moisture-wicking layers. If you’re unsure about specific attire, check the details of your adventure package for clothing recommendations.

Are meals provided during the adventures?

Some of our adventure packages include meals, such as picnic lunches or group dinners, while others may require you to bring your own food. Be sure to check the details of the specific adventure you’ve booked to know if meals are included, and feel free to reach out if you have dietary preferences or restrictions.
