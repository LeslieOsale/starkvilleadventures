Mt. Longonot Hike

Date: 19TH APRIL, 2025
Pick-up Time: 7:00 AM
Destination: Great Rift Valley, Kenya
KSH2500 KSH2000

Why Visit Mt. Longonot?
A Thrilling Adventure: Conquer one of Kenya's most iconic volcanic peaks.
Scenic Views: Enjoy breathtaking views of the Great Rift Valley from the summit.
Nature at its Best: Surreal landscapes and diverse wildlife along the hike.
Trail Highlights
🏞️ Summit Views: Panoramic views of the Great Rift Valley and Lake Naivasha.
🌋 Volcano Crater: Walk around the rim of an active volcanic crater.
🐾 Wildlife Spotting: Watch for local wildlife, including baboons and giraffes.
Difficulty: ⚠️ Challenging
Elevation Gain: A steep climb to 2,776 meters.
Trail Length: ~13 km round trip with challenging ascents.
Terrain: Rocky paths with moderate to steep climbs.
Recommended for experienced hikers.
Essential Information
🛣️ Starting Point: Longonot Gate (near Lake Naivasha)
🧗‍♂️ What to Bring: Sturdy hiking boots, water, snacks, and sun protection.
💬 Fun Fact: Mt. Longonot erupted last around 2.5 million years ago, creating the stunning landscape we see today.
📸 Make sure to snap some photos at the top!
#MtLongonot #RiftValleyHike #KenyaAdventure
